How to use!!!

Choose a Field

You need to choose a attribute from your dataset on which you need to make analysis from the dropdown list and click to analyze

Enter particular attribute

If you want to analyze data on the basics of a particular attribute value you can choose the field and enter its value in the text and click 'Analyze'. This is an optional field, if you leave this empty, entire field will be analyzed on its unique values

Graph Analysis

The field that you are choosing here will be on the X-Axis in further analysis when you choose for graphical analysis.
